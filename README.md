### 🚀 Featured Engineering Projects

#### 1. Autonomous Off-Road Vehicle Architecture (aBAJA)
*Architected a full-scale driverless stack integrating an RPLIDAR A3 and an OAK-D Lite depth camera with an NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano edge-computing platform. Deploys low-latency hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) control loops via an STM32H7 MCU.*

#### 2. EV Powertrain Digital Twin & Energy Optimization
*Developed a high-fidelity physical digital twin of an EV powertrain using **MATLAB/Simulink** (kalman_Filter_3). Uses Extended Kalman Filters to accurately estimate State of Charge (SoC), optimizing energy consumption and dictating modular battery swaps.*

#### 3. Custom RTDS Hardware Architecture (PCB)
*Designed, routed, and fabricated a rugged, 2-layer control board using **KiCad**. Integrated critical safety circuits and low-voltage isolation to deliver a fail-safe central RTDS while minimizing high-current electromagnetic noise.*

#### 4. Live Vehicle Telemetry & DAQ System
*Engineered a multi-sensor data acquisition architecture over a CAN bus utilizing Arduino and ESP32 to capture live dynamic G-forces and steering effort for real-time wireless telemetry.*
